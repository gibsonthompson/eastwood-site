What We Do

Water Heaters 🔥

No hot water? We install, repair, and replace gas, electric, and tankless water heaters — often the same day you call. Serving Gardner, Fitchburg, and all surrounding towns.

Boilers & Furnaces 💨

Your heating system shouldn't be a guessing game. We service oil, gas, and high-efficiency boilers and furnaces — from emergency repairs to full replacements.

Mini Splits & HVAC ❄️

No ductwork? No problem. Ductless mini splits give older New England homes year-round comfort without tearing into walls. We size, install, and service them.

Drain Cleaning 🔧

Slow drains, backed-up sinks, gurgling toilets — we clear blockages in kitchens, bathrooms, and main lines with professional equipment, not chemical band-aids.

Oil to Gas Conversions 🔄

Done scheduling oil deliveries? We handle the full switch to natural gas — permitting, tank removal, new equipment, and final hookup. One contractor, start to finish.

Kitchen & Bath Remodeling 🛁

The plumbing behind the tile matters. We handle rough-ins, fixture installs, tub-to-shower conversions, and gas lines for kitchen and bathroom remodels.
