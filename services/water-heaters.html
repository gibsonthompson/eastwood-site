Signs You Need a New Water Heater

Most tank water heaters last 8 to 12 years. If you're experiencing any of the following, it may be time for a replacement rather than a repair:

  • Water that's rusty, cloudy, or has a metallic taste
  • Rumbling or popping noises from the tank
  • Visible rust or corrosion on the tank
  • Puddles or moisture around the base of the unit
  • Hot water runs out faster than it used to
  • Your unit is over 10 years old

We carry units from trusted manufacturers and can often complete a full water heater replacement the same day you call. Every installation includes proper permitting, code-compliant connections, and disposal of your old unit.

Water Heater FAQ

How much does a water heater replacement cost in Gardner, MA?

The cost depends on the type and size of unit. A standard 40-50 gallon tank water heater replacement typically runs between $1,200 and $2,500 installed. Tankless units cost more upfront but save on energy long-term. We provide free estimates before any work begins.

How long does a water heater installation take?

A straightforward tank-to-tank replacement usually takes 2 to 4 hours. Switching from tank to tankless may take a full day due to additional gas line and venting work. We'll give you a clear timeline before we start.

Should I switch to a tankless water heater?

Tankless water heaters provide hot water on demand and use less energy than tank models. They're a good fit if you have a larger household or want to free up space. We can help you weigh the pros and cons for your specific situation.
