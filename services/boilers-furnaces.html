Repair vs. Replace — We'll Give It to You Straight

Not every broken furnace needs to be replaced. Sometimes a $300 ignitor swap buys you another 5 years. Other times, dumping $800 into a 20-year-old boiler is throwing money away. We'll look at the age, condition, repair history, and efficiency of your system and tell you what we'd do if it were our house.

If replacement is the right call, upgrading to a high-efficiency system can cut your heating bills by 20-30%. That matters in a place where heating season runs from October through April.

Annual Maintenance Keeps You Out of Trouble

The best time to find a problem with your boiler is in September — not January. We do annual tune-ups that include a full safety check, cleaning, and efficiency test. It's the cheapest insurance you can buy against a mid-winter breakdown.

Boiler & Furnace FAQ

How do I know if my furnace needs to be replaced?

If it's over 15-20 years old, needs repairs more than once a season, or your heating bills keep climbing even though you haven't changed anything — it's probably time. We'll look at it and give you an honest assessment.

Do you service oil boilers?

Yes — oil, gas, propane, and high-efficiency systems. If you're thinking about switching from oil to gas, we do those conversions too.

How often should I have my heating system serviced?

Once a year, before heating season starts. September and October are ideal. Annual maintenance catches problems early and keeps your warranty valid.

What towns do you serve?

Gardner, Fitchburg, Leominster, Westminster, Ashburnham, Templeton, Winchendon, Hubbardston, Athol — all of North Central Massachusetts.
