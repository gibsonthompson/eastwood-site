Boiler & Furnace FAQ

How often should I service my boiler or furnace?

At minimum once a year, ideally before heating season. Annual maintenance catches worn parts, improves efficiency, and prevents mid-winter breakdowns. It also keeps your manufacturer warranty valid.

Should I repair or replace my old boiler?

If your boiler is under 15 years old and the repair cost is under half the price of a new unit, repair usually makes sense. Beyond that, a high-efficiency replacement will pay for itself in lower heating bills within a few years.

Do you service oil boilers?

Yes. We service oil, gas, and high-efficiency systems. If you're considering switching from oil to gas, we handle those conversions as well — see our oil to gas conversion page for details.

What areas do you serve for heating service?

We serve Gardner, Fitchburg, Leominster, Westminster, Ashburnham, Templeton, Winchendon, Hubbardston, Athol, and all of North Central Massachusetts.
