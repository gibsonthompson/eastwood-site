Drain Cleaning Services

Clogged drains are one of the most common plumbing issues homeowners deal with. Hair, grease, soap buildup, food waste, and tree root intrusion can all cause slow or completely blocked drains. Store-bought drain cleaners often make things worse by corroding your pipes without actually solving the problem.

At Eastwood Plumbing, we use professional-grade equipment to clear blockages the right way. We serve homes and businesses in Gardner, Fitchburg, Leominster, Westminster, Templeton, Winchendon, and the surrounding area.

We clear clogs in:

  • Kitchen sinks and garbage disposals
  • Bathroom sinks, tubs, and showers
  • Toilets and floor drains
  • Main sewer lines
  • Laundry drains
  • Basement drains

When to Call a Professional

If a plunger doesn't solve the problem, or if you're dealing with recurring clogs in the same drain, there's likely a deeper issue. Grease buildup in kitchen lines, tree root intrusion in main sewer lines, and deteriorating cast iron pipes are all problems that require professional equipment and experience to diagnose and fix properly.

We'll tell you exactly what's causing the problem and what it'll take to fix it — no surprises on the bill.

Drain Cleaning FAQ

How much does drain cleaning cost?

Simple clogs typically cost between $150 and $300 to clear. Main sewer line work costs more depending on the severity and access. We give you a price before we start.

Are chemical drain cleaners safe to use?

We don't recommend them. Products like Drano can corrode pipes over time, especially in older homes with cast iron or galvanized piping. Professional mechanical clearing is safer and more effective.

Why does my drain keep clogging?

Recurring clogs usually point to a deeper issue — grease buildup in kitchen lines, hair and soap accumulation in bathroom drains, or tree root intrusion in your main sewer line. We can diagnose the root cause and fix it permanently.
