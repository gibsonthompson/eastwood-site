The Plumbing Side of Your Remodel

Tile, paint, and fixtures get all the attention — but the plumbing behind the walls is what actually makes a bathroom or kitchen work. Bad connections leak. Undersized supply lines kill water pressure. Improperly vented drains gurgle and smell. We make sure none of that happens, so your investment actually lasts.

We handle everything plumbing-related in a remodel:

  • Full bathroom remodels — tub-to-shower conversions, walk-in showers, double vanities
  • Kitchen sink, dishwasher, and ice maker plumbing
  • Fixture upgrades — faucets, showerheads, toilets
  • Rough-in plumbing for new layouts
  • Gas line installation for ranges and cooktops
  • Water line relocation and re-piping
  • Shut-off valve replacement and supply upgrades

Remodeling FAQ

Do you handle the whole remodel or just plumbing?

Just the plumbing — rough-in, fixtures, gas lines, supply and drain work. We partner with GCs for the rest, or can recommend trusted contractors in the Gardner area if you need one.

How much does remodeling plumbing cost?

A straightforward fixture swap (same locations) runs $1,500 to $3,000. Moving fixtures to new locations involves more pipe work — typically $3,000 to $6,000+. We scope and quote every project before starting.

Can you convert my bathtub to a walk-in shower?

Yes — it's one of our most popular projects. We relocate the drain, install new supply lines, and get everything prepped for tile. Typically a 1 to 2 day plumbing job.

Do I need a permit for bathroom remodeling in Gardner?

Plumbing work that involves moving or adding fixtures typically requires a permit in Massachusetts. We pull all necessary permits and schedule inspections — it's included in the job.
