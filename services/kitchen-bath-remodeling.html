Remodeling Services

A kitchen or bathroom remodel is one of the best investments you can make in your home. But the plumbing work needs to be done right — bad rough-in work leads to leaks, poor water pressure, and code violations that come back to bite you during a home sale. At Eastwood Plumbing, we make sure the plumbing behind your walls is as solid as the finishes you see.

We handle all plumbing-related remodeling work:

  • Full bathroom remodels — tub-to-shower conversions, walk-in showers, double vanities
  • Kitchen sink and dishwasher plumbing
  • Fixture upgrades — faucets, showerheads, toilets
  • Rough-in plumbing for new layouts
  • Gas line installation for ranges and cooktops
  • Water line relocation and re-piping
  • Shut-off valve replacement and water supply upgrades

Remodeling FAQ

Do you do the full remodel or just the plumbing?

We focus on the plumbing — rough-in, fixtures, gas lines, and water supply. We work alongside your GC or can recommend trusted contractors in the Gardner area for tile, cabinets, and finishes.

How much does bathroom plumbing cost for a remodel?

Plumbing for a straightforward fixture swap (same locations) typically runs $1,500 to $3,000. Moving fixtures to new locations involves more pipe work and can run $3,000 to $6,000+. We scope every project individually and quote before starting.

Can you convert my tub to a walk-in shower?

Yes. Tub-to-shower conversions are one of our most common remodeling projects. We handle the plumbing and drain work, and can coordinate with a tile installer for the finished product.
